Personality Development

Definition of personality – Personality is a characteristic way of thinking, feeling, behaving, and expressing. Personality development is described as improving and grooming one's outer and inner self to make a positive difference in one's life. It can be done in many ways as there can be many ways to improve an individual’s personality. Personality development can be done in many ways-

  • physical
  • spiritual
  • mental
  • physical
  • emotional

Physical personality development

Physical characteristics are defining traits or features of a person's body. They encompass any way you can describe the physical traits of a person or group of people based on what you can visually observe. The first thing you see when you look at someone could be their hair, clothes, nose, or figure. As it is rightly said, the physical characteristics of a person define their physical personality and it is very important to develop a physical personality. Developing your physical personality not only draws the attention of other people towards you but also, keeps you healthy. It makes you gain confidence and look good.

Factors representing lack of physical development-

There are many factors that can tell that a person is not physically developed-

  • Being overweight.
  • Not having good behaviour that reduces the personality grades.
  • Unhealthy or suffering from any disease.
  • Not having good communication.
  • Laziness or being inactive.

Ways to enhance physical personality

  • Eating healthy.
  • Regular envolvement in sports and exercises.
  • Having good communication skills to improve your personality.
  • Avoiding junk food.
  • Meditate for better clarity and vision.

Intellectual personality development

Mental or Intellectual development includes such abilities as attending, peerceiving, observing, remembering, imagining, thinking, solving problems, and growth of intelligence as well as language. These abilities change, grow and mature with age and decline in old age. This development is important as it helps a person in their carrier and prevents them from becoming an emotional fool. The factors that affect mental development include maturation, learning, and education.

Ways to enhance intellectual personality

  • Get enough sleep for the proper functioning of the brain.
  • Try to have a regular balanced schedule.
  • Eat healthy food and exercise with meditation.
  • Solve puzzles or games and listen to soft and pleasing music.
  • Read to have exposure and gain knowledge about different topics.
  • Socialize yourself and know about different people.
  • Come out of your comfort zone and learn about life in a more practical way.

Social personality development

Social development is about improving the well-being of every individual in society so they can reach their full potential. The success of a society is linked to the well-being of each and every citizen. Social development means investing in people. Social development is the change over time in an individual's understanding of, attitudes concerning, and behavior toward others; for example, a developmental change in how people behave with members of the other gender or their understanding of what friendship entails. Ultimately, we all have to live with different people in society. We have to interact and communicate with different people in society. It is very important to develop socially and build good relations with society. We cannot live without society as everyone needs some people for being accompanied and it is very important to have good behavior towards other people in the surrounding and environment.

Ways to enhance social personality

  • Have good communication skills.
  • Avoid being aggressive.
  • Have good listening capability and skills.
  • Respect towards culture and differences of other people in society.
  • Curiosity to interact with new people.
  • Give time and space to other people.
  • Have an attractive physical and intellectual personality.

Spiritual personality development

Spiritual personality development is the most important as it tells a person about their weakness and strength. Your Spiritual Personality takes the study of personality to its logical and important next step: helping you to make connections between the strengths and weaknesses of your personality and your pursuit of a relationship with God. Spiritual personality development is more important as it brings peace inside your soul and brings trust whenever there is any problem that you can’t solve. A spiritually developed person will have faith in an unknown authority and prevent him from losing hope.

Ways to enhance spiritual personality

  • Meditation
  • Have gratitude towards other people.
  • Praying and connecting with god.
  • Be thankful to god.
  • Being kind and helpful.

Emotional personality development

Emotional development involves learning what feelings and emotions are, understanding how and why they occur, recognizing your own feelings and those of others, and developing effective ways of managing those feelings. A well-developed emotional person has a very good balance of emotions and avoids an overflow of emotions. Sometimes, an overflow of emotions makes a person an emotional fool with no intelligence. This development allows a person to have a good balance in emotional, happiness, anger, and expression levels. One should have a good balance between intelligence and emotions as both of them are interrelated. Emotional personality development involves- Balancing different emotions, Self-motivation, Self-awareness,and Mentoring the levels of emotions.

Ways to enhance emotional personality

  • Identify and accept your emotions.
  • Have no guilt for your emotions.
  • Have a strong belief in yourself.
  • Manage negative emotions and spread positivity.
  • Value yourself.
  • Have a good balance between your mind and heart.

Why the personality of a person should be developed?

There are many advantages of having a good personality-

  • They are sincere.
  • They are understanding and interesting.
  • They are curious and have a positive approach.
  • They are open minded and confident.
  • They have good listening skills and are attentive.
  • They are healthy.
  • They are encouraging.

Personality development is very important as it will change you in a positive direction. It will develop you mentally, emotionally, socially, and physically. If you will not develop yourself then you will remain the same. Sometimes, change is necessary as it will make you more productive and Qualitative.

"Sometime, change leads to development."
