WHAT IS INTERNSHIP ?

Dictionary.com defines an internship as “any official or formal program to provide practical experience for beginners in an occupation or profession.” The most important element of internships is the integration of classroom knowledge and theory with practical application and skills. Student interns gain this experience in either professional or community settings.

IMPORTANCE OF INTERNSHIP FOR ENGINEEERING STUDENT: -

An internship enables you to gain some first-hand exposure of working in the real world. Not only do internships help develop your professionalism, but they also encourage character growth.  Having an internship benefits you in the working environment, and it also builds your professional network. If you are an engineering student then along with your college studies you have to work on your technical skills and practical knowledge. internship gives a practical approach and helps to gain industrial experience. It enables the students to develop a professional aptitude and broaden the spectrum of knowledge and experience.

TYPES OF INTERNSHIPS

There are so many types of internships:

  • Paid Internships
  • Full Time internship
  • Summer internships
  • Field Experience
  • Externships
  • Virtual Internships
